Versions

We are providing two versions of the application. The STANDARD version and the PREMIUM version. The Premium features can be purchased within the app through in-app purchase. Most of the features can be availed in the FREE version. But some of the features like creating backup in Google drive or saving reports as PDF or XLS is only available in the PREMIUM version. Here is a detailed comparision of the STANDARD and the PREMIUM version.

STANDARD

All the features mentioned below are available for FREE

  • Add expense
  • Add income
  • Add transfer from one account to another
  • 3 Accounts
  • One budget
  • Expense and Income reports
  • Schedule expense
  • Account reports
  • Graphical dislay of reports
  • Schedule expense
  • Closing and Opening balance of Accounts
  • Customize dashboard
  • Automatic backup in device
  • Password protection
  • and many more

PREMIUM

Apart from the features mentioned in STANDARD version, below features are available in PREMIUM version

  • Export reports as PDF and XLS
  • Fingerprint password
  • Data backup in Google drive
  • Synchronize backup in multiple devices
  • Unlimited Accounts
  • Unlimited Budgets
  • Analytics report for all expense categories
  • Event and Event reports
  • Bill reminder
  • Easy data import from device and Google drive
  • Remove advertisement
